    No.1

    NOODLE with TAMARI

    1. Slice beef into very thin strips.
    2. Mix cornstarch, broth, soy and sugar until smooth. Set aside.
    3. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in saucepot or wok over high heat.
      Add beef in 2 batches and stir-fry until browned. Set beef aside.
    4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in saucepot or wok over high heat.
      Add beef in 2 batches and stir-fry until browned. Set beef aside.
    5. Add 1 tablespoon oil. Add the mushrooms, cabbage, peppers,
      celery and green onions in 2 batches and stir-fry over medium
      heat until tender-crisp. Set vegetables aside.
    6. Stir cornstarch mixture and add.
      Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring constantly.
      Return beef and vegetables to saucepot and heat through.
      Serve over rice.

    No.2

    Oyakodon

    1. Prepare the ingredients: Cut the onion, mitsuba/green onions,
      and chicken and crack the eggs.
    2. Cook the onion in the seasoning mixture.
    3. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink.
    4. Drizzle egg mixture at two separate times. Garnish mitsuba/green onions.
    5. Spoon the finished dish over the bed of steamed rice in a donburi bowl.

    No.3

    Zosui (Japanese Rice Soup)

    1. Make dashi.
    2. Cut all the ingredients and rinse the cooked rice.
    3. Start cooking chicken, followed by dense vegetables, mushrooms, and rice.
    4. Once everything is cooked through, drizzle eggs and sprinkle green onions.
    5. Serve warm!
